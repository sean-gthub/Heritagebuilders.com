Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my property qualifies as historic?

Properties typically qualify if they're at least 50 years old and retain historic integrity. We offer free preliminary assessments to determine eligibility for local, state, or national historic registers.

What's the typical timeline for a preservation project?

Timelines vary based on project scope, but most complete restorations take 6-18 months. Smaller projects like window restoration may take 4-8 weeks. We provide detailed schedules after assessment.

Are there tax incentives for historic preservation?

Yes! Many jurisdictions offer tax credits for certified historic properties. We help clients navigate these programs, which can cover 20-40% of qualified rehabilitation costs.

Do you work with historic preservation consultants?

Absolutely. We collaborate with architectural historians, conservators, and engineers to ensure historically appropriate and structurally sound preservation solutions.
