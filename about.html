Our Heritage

Founded in 1980 by master carpenter Elias Whitmore, Heritage Builders began as a small workshop specializing in traditional building techniques. What started as a family business restoring historic homes in New England has grown into a nationally recognized leader in architectural preservation.

Through five generations, we've maintained our commitment to craftsmanship while adapting to new conservation technologies. Our team of 56 skilled artisans combines time-honored methods with modern preservation science to protect our architectural legacy.
